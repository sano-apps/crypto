App Privacy Policy -- Updated 30 May 2023
----------------------------------------------------------

Thank you for using this app. 
Your privacy is important to us, and we want to make sure you understand how we handle your data.

**Information Collection and Usage:**

Our app does not collect any personal information about you. 
We do not gather, use, or share any data with third parties. 
Your usage of our app is completely anonymous.

**Crash Data Collection:**

When you use our app, we may collect crash data to improve app stability and performance. 
This crash data includes technical information related to app crashes, 
such as error logs and diagnostic details. 
Please note that this data is collected and processed anonymously, 
without any personally identifiable information. It is used solely for identifying and 
addressing issues to enhance the user experience.

**No Cryptographic Data Collection:**

We want to assure you that we do not collect any cryptographic data, 
such as encryption keys, passwords, or sensitive security information.

**Your Consent:**

By using our application, you acknowledge and agree to the collection and processing 
of crash data as described above. 
Your continued use of the app constitutes your consent to this data collection.

**Data Security:**

We take privacy and data security seriously. We have implemented measures to 
safeguard the collected crash data and ensure it is used only for the purposes 
described in this policy.

**Changes to this Policy:**

This privacy policy may be subject to changes or updates. 
Please check this page for the latest version to stay informed about how we protect your privacy.

**Contact Us:**

If you have any questions or concerns about our app's privacy practices, 
please feel free to [contact us](mailto:privacy@sanoapps.com).

If you do not agree with this policy, we kindly request that you discontinue the use of our application.

Thank you for trusting us with your privacy.
